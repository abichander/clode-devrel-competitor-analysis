# Competitor Research – Clode

> Reference product: **Clode**  
> Category: AI-powered cloud automation & platform engineering  
> Focus: Infrastructure automation, SDLC operations, execution-first platform

---

## 1. Netlify

- **Website:** https://www.netlify.com  
- **Founder:** Mathias Biilmann  
- **Location:** US  
- **Category:** Platform Engineering / Frontend DevOps  

### What they do  
Netlify provides a platform to build, deploy, and host modern web applications with CI/CD baked in.

### Target users  
Frontend developers, full-stack developers, startups, SMBs

### Key features
- Git-based CI/CD
- Serverless functions
- Edge deployments
- Preview deploys

### Positioning  
“Build, deploy, and scale modern web apps.”

### Overlap with Clode  
Deployment automation and infrastructure abstraction.

### How Clode is different  
Clode focuses on backend infrastructure, operations, and lifecycle automation beyond deployment.

### DevRel angle  
Frontend DevOps vs full platform and infra automation.

### Content hooks
- Frontend DevOps is not platform engineering  
- Deployment is only a part of automation

## Final Takeaway

Most competitors focus on:
- Visibility (dashboards, catalogs)
- Narrow slices of the SDLC
- Heavy enterprise tooling

**Clode’s differentiation:**  
Execution-first, AI-driven automation across infrastructure and operations.

This positions Clode strongly in the modern platform engineering ecosystem.
